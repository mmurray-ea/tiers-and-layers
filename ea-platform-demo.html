Phase 1 · BRM · Step 3 of 4
Capabilities
What does the business do? Classify each capability as Differentiating, Core operational, or Shared/support. This is the most strategically important question in the entire process.
Differentiating capabilities are your competitive advantage — things competitors cannot easily replicate. Be deliberate. Most capabilities are Core or Shared.

Phase 2 · TRM · Step 1 of 4
Systems of Record
For each business object, identify the one authoritative system that owns it. Each object MUST have exactly one system of record — this is a governed Rule.
Rule: Each business object MUST have exactly one system of record. Multiple SoR assignments are a governance violation requiring an exception.

Phase 2 · TRM · Step 3 of 4
Business Services
Identify the applications or services that implement each Core capability. These sit in the Service tier and are the bridge between capabilities and systems of record.
Rule: Business logic MUST reside in the Service tier. It MUST NOT reside in the Integration or UI & Interfaces tiers.
